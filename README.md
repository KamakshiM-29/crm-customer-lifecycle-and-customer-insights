# Customer Relationship Management: Customer Lifecycle, Loyalty & Competitive Insights

## Overview

Strong customer relationships are built through continuous engagement, personalized experiences, and an understanding of customer needs throughout their lifecycle. This project explores core Customer Relationship Management (CRM) concepts through a practical case study focused on customer lifecycle management, customer loyalty measurement, and competitor benchmarking.

The project combines CRM theory with customer research by evaluating customer satisfaction using Net Promoter Score (NPS), analyzing subscription strategies across leading publishing brands, and identifying opportunities to improve customer retention and long-term customer value.

---

## Business Objective

The objective of this project was to understand how organizations can strengthen customer relationships by:

- Understanding the customer lifecycle and Customer Lifetime Value (CLV)
- Measuring customer loyalty using Net Promoter Score (NPS)
- Designing a customer feedback survey
- Benchmarking competitor subscription and promotional strategies
- Drawing customer insights that support better retention and engagement decisions

## Methodology

The project was completed using a structured research approach.

### CRM Research

- Studied customer lifecycle management and CRM frameworks
- Evaluated the relationship between customer retention and long-term business growth

### Competitor Analysis

Compared subscription offerings, pricing strategies, and promotional campaigns across multiple publishing brands including:

- Outlook India
- The Hindu
- India Today
- Times of India
- Business Today
- Business Standard
- Economic Times
- Mint
- Moneycontrol
- Outlook Traveller
- Conde Nast Traveller India
- Financial Express

### Customer Research

- Designed an NPS survey using Google Forms
- Collected customer responses
- Categorized respondents into Promoters, Passives, and Detractors
- Calculated Net Promoter Scores for multiple brands
- Interpreted customer loyalty trends using survey findings

---

## Key Insights

Some of the major observations from the study include:

- Customer loyalty varies significantly across brands despite offering similar subscription models.
- Subscription pricing alone does not determine customer advocacy or satisfaction.
- Effective customer retention strategies contribute more to long-term customer value than acquisition-focused initiatives.
- Net Promoter Score provides a simple but effective framework for identifying customer loyalty and potential churn risks.
- Competitor benchmarking helps identify opportunities to improve customer experience and strengthen retention strategies.
